Roblox is an online platform and game creation system where users can play a massive variety of user-created games and experiences. It allows users to create their own games using the free platform tool, Roblox Studio, and share them with others. Users can access the platform on a variety of devices, including computers, smartphones, and gaming consoles. Key features User-generated content: The platform's content is primarily created by its users, resulting in a vast and constantly growing library of different game genres.

A vatar customization: Users can customize their personal avatars with a wide range of free and purchasable items. Social interaction: Roblox includes social features where players can chat with friends and other users, acting as a social media platform as well as a gaming one. Cross-platform availability: Players can access and play on various devices like smartphones, tablets, PCs, and game consoles. In-game currency: While many games are free to play, some offer in-game purchases using a virtual currency called Robux. Game development: Roblox Studio is a separate tool that enables users to design and publish their own games, which can help teach basic coding and game design
